Iconic Wins and Moments

India’s first ICC World Cup victory in 1983, under the leadership of Kapil Dev, was a watershed moment. It not only put Indian cricket on the global map but also inspired a generation.

The T20 World Cup win in 2007, led by MS Dhoni, marked a new era, showcasing India’s potential in the shortest format. The 2011 World Cup win on home soil remains etched in every fan’s memory.

Other historic moments include the famous Kolkata Test win against Australia in 2001, Sachin Tendulkar’s 100 international centuries, and Virat Kohli’s dominance across formats.
