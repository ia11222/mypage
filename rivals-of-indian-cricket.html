Epic Rivalries

Indian cricket has had several fierce rivalries over the decades, none greater than with Pakistan. The Indo-Pak cricket clashes are more than just games; they’re high-stakes events filled with drama and intensity.

Other notable rivalries include the battles with Australia, especially during the Border-Gavaskar Trophy, and the historic encounters with England, dating back to India's first Test in 1932.

These rivalries have defined Indian cricket’s growth, pushing the team to greater heights.
