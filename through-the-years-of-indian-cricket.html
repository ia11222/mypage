India’s Journey in Cricket

The journey of Indian cricket began in 1932, with their debut Test against England. Over the decades, the team transitioned from underdogs to world champions, capturing the imagination of millions.

From the emergence of Sunil Gavaskar in the 1970s to the dominance of the 2010s under Virat Kohli, Indian cricket has constantly evolved. Today, it boasts a perfect blend of experience and youth, ensuring a bright future.

Players like Virender Sehwag (fastest centuries) and Anil Kumble (most Test wickets for India) have set records that highlight India’s growth and dominance in world cricket.
