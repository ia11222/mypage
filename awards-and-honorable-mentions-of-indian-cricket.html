Recognitions and Honors

Indian cricket has been decorated with countless awards over the years. Players like Sachin Tendulkar (Bharat Ratna) and MS Dhoni (Padma Bhushan) have been recognized for their exceptional contributions.

The ICC has awarded India multiple accolades, including ODI and Test team rankings, highlighting its dominance in world cricket. Special mentions go to young stars like Rishabh Pant and Shubman Gill, who represent the future.
